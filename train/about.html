About Us

India is a crowed country; in this busy life cycle everyone is busy and want things faster. Also, India has the largest railway network in the whole world and it is so much difficult to handle such a large system manually. besides we can see such a big queue to the ticket counters, sometimes we missed our train for such a long queue. to process our work faster we introduce “ ” that time compressing and easy to book your tickets. There are so many websites or apps from where you can reserve your tickets for long journey but a few apps that can book ticket for local trains. This system is basically concerned with the booking of local railway tickets to the passenger. the need of this system arouses by computerizing it, it become possible to overcome the limitations and make the system operations more efficient.

The entire application is web based. The front-end has been built using HTML and CSS while the back- end uses PHP and has connectivity with MySQL. Prolonged study of the functionalities of the railway system and its specific requirement has given it a wonderful shape both technically and usability wise. It covers required modules right from daily journey experience .
